Did you know the Akan people of Ghana name their children after the day of the week they were born?

This web application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and if they were born in Ghana, shows their equivalent Akan name

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born
